The Spellcaster has two attack methods: Direct Killing & Spelling. Pressing the Kill button twice (Depending on Switch Method) will result in a normal kill. Pressing the Kill button once will cast a spell on the Spellcasterâ€™s target. During the Meeting, the Spellcasters target(s) will have a symbol next to their name (A cross) indicating that they were Spelled. If the Spellcaster is not ejected/guessed by the end of the meeting, all of their targets will die when the Meeting concludes.

* Max
  * Set the Maximum amount of Spellcasters that can be in a match
* Switch Action Via
  * Set how the Spellcaster can switch between using a Spell, and killing normally
    * Kill - Alternate between normal killing and Spellcasting when interacting with the Kill button on a target
    * Vent - Alternate between normal killing and Spellcasting when interacting with a Vent
    * Double-Click - Single click to Spellcast, double click to kill normally
